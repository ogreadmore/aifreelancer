Frequently asked questions

We help small and mid‑sized businesses deploy practical AI that delivers measurable ROI — increasing revenue, reducing costs, and freeing teams to focus on strategic work. We emphasize fast pilots, clear KPIs, and long‑term maintainability.
Companies with recurring operational work, customer interactions, or data-rich processes — typically mid-market firms with clear revenue or cost KPIs. We work well with teams ready to measure impact and integrate solutions into existing workflows.
We begin with a discovery call and a short, focused impact assessment. Most clients start with a 4–8 week pilot proving value; successful pilots move to phased rollouts with monitoring, governance and optimization.
Pilot projects typically deliver measurable wins within 4–8 weeks. Time to full production depends on integration complexity and scale, but we focus on quick, high‑impact milestones first.
Engagements are scoped to outcomes: fixed-price pilots, monthly retainers for ongoing work, or blended models. Typical pilot investments start in the low five-figure range — contact us for a tailored estimate.
We apply industry best practices: least privilege access, anonymization where possible, vendor assessments, and clear data retention policies. We can work within your security controls and sign NDAs or data processing agreements.
