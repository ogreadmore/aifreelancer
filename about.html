The Origin

Why this company exists.

My parents were small business owners. I grew up watching them compete — not just against other small businesses, but against industries increasingly shaped by enterprise-scale tools, budgets, and platforms they had no realistic path to accessing.

I spent the early part of my career in web development, fintech, and financial services. I built systems inside large institutions. I saw firsthand what those advantages looked like from the inside — and how wide the gap really was.

AI is the first technology I've seen that gives smaller businesses a genuine path to closing that gap without enterprise-scale resources. That's what led me to build AI Freelancer — not any particular fondness for AI in itself, but a genuine belief that it's the most powerful tool available right now for leveling the playing field for SMBs.

Technology should increase the agency of ordinary people and smaller organizations — not just amplify the dominance of already-centralized power.
Taylor Oliphant — Founder

On what makes this work meaningful

Mission

Decentralize economic power. One SMB at a time.

AI Freelancer helps small and midsize businesses harness AI, automation, and practical digital systems to drive real results — without needing enterprise budgets, giant teams, or technical complexity. We believe the power of advanced technology should not be limited to the largest companies on earth.

How We Work

Performance first.
AI is the tool.

01

We sell outcomes, not AI

AI is a means to an end. We only recommend what we're confident will actually move the needle — not whatever is newest or trendiest. Our clients succeed because we're honest about what works and what doesn't.

02

Every engagement is custom

The most valuable solutions are almost always highly specific to how a particular business actually operates. We get into the weeds of each client's workflows, systems, and real problems — not a template version of them.

03

We think in Type 2

Most of what we do is slow, effortful, conscious problem-solving — not pattern-matching on autopilot. Every engagement requires thinking hard from the start, because the problems are new and the combinations are always different.

04

Full-service, end-to-end

We plan, staff, build, and automate across every function a business needs: websites, HR, operations, marketing, training, and more. Most SMBs can't afford the end-to-end support large companies take for granted. We exist to change that.

05

Speed without sacrifice

We use AI at every stage to keep costs down and quality up. Real results in days or weeks — not the months-long engagements that price small businesses out before anything ships.

06

Independence as the goal

We're not trying to create dependency on us. We're trying to give smaller companies more leverage, more independence, and a better chance to thrive in a world where scale is increasingly concentrated.
