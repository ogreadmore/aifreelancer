Step 1 ‚Äî Kickoff & Metrics

Newbie guide: Kickoff

Story mode: Write the rules and collect the keys.

  1. Define the numbers: ‚ÄúLead = contact with email,‚Äù ‚ÄúNew lead = first seen date.‚Äù
  2. List sources: Website form, CRM, Ads, Web analytics, Billing.
  3. Ask for read‚Äëonly access (or CSV exports). Save them in a secure place.
  4. Agree on KPI targets for this phase (freshness, dedupe, routing accuracy).

Step 2 ‚Äî Ingest & Upsert

Newbie guide: Upserts

Story mode: Never duplicate a person ‚Äî update them.

  1. Pick your key: email (lowercased) and company domain.
  2. Schedule jobs hourly. First run does a full load; later runs only new/changed rows.
  3. After each run, check the log: start time, rows read, rows inserted, rows updated.
  4. If counts look strange, pause and compare against the source export before proceeding.

Step 3 ‚Äî Normalize & Dedupe

Newbie guide: Cleaning data

Story mode: Make messy fields neat and consistent.

  1. Phones: convert to E.164 (e.g., +14155552671). Reject obviously invalid numbers.
  2. Emails: lowercase and trim. Split domain part for company matching.
  3. Deduplicate: group by email and then by domain; keep the newest record; merge fields.
  4. UTMs: create a mapping table that converts messy values to a short list (email/paid/organic/referral).

Step 4 ‚Äî Owner & Alerts

Newbie guide: Alerts

Story mode: Right person, right time, right message.

  1. Pick the channel (Slack, Email). Add the team or owner users.
  2. Write a short template: name, email, source, assigned owner, and a link.
  3. Set quiet hours (e.g., 8pm‚Äì8am) and an escalation path for urgent items.
  4. Send one test alert and confirm the owner can click through to the record.

Step 5 ‚Äî Email Auth & Sequences

Newbie guide: Deliverability

Story mode: Set three DNS records and keep emails human.

  1. Add SPF and DKIM records from your email provider to your domain‚Äôs DNS.
  2. Add DMARC with a conservative policy (p=none) to start; collect reports.
  3. Write 2‚Äì3 short, on‚Äëbrand templates and include an easy opt‚Äëout.
  4. Send a test to yourself; check it lands in Inbox; adjust if needed.

Step 6 ‚Äî Dashboards & Hypercare

Newbie guide: Hypercare

Story mode: Launch small, watch closely, then expand.

  1. Share the dashboard with 3‚Äì5 people first; collect comments.
  2. Watch freshness and row counts daily; fix any gaps immediately.
  3. When stable for 48 hours, roll out to the rest of the team.

Step 7 ‚Äî Ongoing Optimization

Newbie guide: Keep it sharp

Story mode: Small weekly habits keep data trustworthy.

  1. Pick 10 random records; confirm owner, UTM, and phone format are correct.
  2. Fix one rule per week (or confirm no change needed). Document the change.
  3. Once a month, post a 1‚Äëpage KPI snapshot and the top 3 improvements.

Step 1 ‚Äî Workflow Design

  1. Pick features: work email, industry fit, page views, firmographic size.
  2. Assign points (e.g., work email +10, visited pricing +5).
