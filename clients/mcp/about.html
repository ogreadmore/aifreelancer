About Dan

Built around recovery, privacy, and consistency.

Dan came into cryotherapy through a practical interest in the healing arts, bodywork, and recovery tools people can actually use. After early health and wellness coursework at community college, he kept building through certification-based training focused on cryotherapy, body contouring, and client care.

That path turned into a mobile practice designed to feel more personal than a typical clinic visit. The goal is simple: show up prepared, keep the experience private, and make each appointment feel clear from start to finish.

Background

  • Founder and operator of Mobile Cryo Pro
  • Community-college wellness studies with continuing certification training
  • Focused on localized cryotherapy, CryoSkin, cryofacials, and private appointments
  • Modern equipment, clean setup standards, and mobile convenience

What Clients Can Expect

  • Clear service options and realistic guidance
  • Targeted treatment instead of vague wellness language
  • Clean equipment setup and efficient appointment flow
  • A mobile format that respects the client's time
