Mobile visits

What a mobile recovery visit looks like.

The setup is intentionally simple: a quiet corner, a power outlet, a quick intake, and a focused plan for the people who booked time.

May 2026 4 min read

The short version

We bring the setup. You provide a small private area and access to power. The visit stays quiet, clean, and efficient.

Book a Visit

A mobile recovery visit should not interrupt the rhythm of the gym, office, hotel, or event. The whole point is to bring a professional recovery setup into a familiar space and keep it easy for everyone involved.

Before the visit.

We confirm the address, parking, timing, and the number of people expected. If the visit is for a team, group, or event, we also talk through the flow so people are not waiting around.

What we need from the space.

A power outlet and a quiet corner are enough for most visits. Privacy matters, especially for body contouring or targeted treatment areas, so a side room or screened area is ideal.

Typical setup time:

About ten minutes once we are inside. We bring the equipment, treatment supplies, cleaning materials, and the intake process.

How the session flows.

Each person gets a quick check-in before treatment starts. We ask what they are working on, what feels irritated, and whether anything should be avoided. Then we treat the target area and make simple recommendations for the rest of the day.

Why gyms like it.

It gives members access to recovery support without sending them elsewhere. It also works well for pop-ups, training groups, wellness days, and private events where convenience matters.
