Where things stand We are exploring future territories carefully—not handing out a one-size-fits-all package.

The mobile model

Meet clients where they already are.

Instead of relying on a traditional storefront, Mobile Cryo Pro can serve homes, gyms, offices, hotels, events, recovery facilities, and equine properties. The model is flexible by design and built around strong local relationships.

Who may be a fit

Good operators matter more than a perfect résumé.

We want to hear from people who care about client service, professionalism, and building something useful in their community. Experience in wellness, fitness, sports, healthcare, hospitality, or business ownership can help, but cryotherapy experience is not necessarily required.

Support in development

Build with a playbook, not from scratch.

Future partners may receive guidance around equipment, initial training, treatment protocols, operating standards, marketing, website support, territory planning, and the day-to-day lessons learned from the Northern California operation.

How we plan to grow

Carefully, with quality first.

The goal is not to grow as fast as possible. It is to find the right markets and people, protect the customer experience, and make sure each future operator represents the brand well.
