The basics

How this all works.

Do you offer whole-body chamber cryotherapy?

No. Mobile Cryo Pro focuses on localized cryotherapy and CryoSkin 3.0. We can treat more than one area in a visit, but we do not use a whole-body chamber.

What does a visit actually look like?

Dan shows up, sets up in about ten minutes, and we have a quick chat about what you’re working on. Treatment itself takes 15–45 minutes depending on the service. You’re back to your day right after.

What safety screening do you do?

We do a quick intake before treatment to flag anything that needs extra care, including cold sensitivity, certain circulatory conditions, and pregnancy. When a request falls outside our lane, we will ask you to check with your doctor first.

Recovery

For sore bodies.

What can it help with?

Sore shoulders, knees, hips, backs, elbows, feet. Plantar fasciitis, tennis elbow, rotator-cuff flare-ups, post-workout soreness—the usual suspects that get in the way of training and daily life.

How fast will I feel something?

Response varies by person, area, and goal. Dan can explain what is realistic for a single visit and when a focused series may make more sense.

Does this replace PT or medical care?

Not at all. We pair well with physical therapy, chiropractic, massage, and your regular care team. If something’s outside our lane, we’ll tell you.

CryoSkin & cryofacials

For contouring and face goals.

What can CryoSkin 3.0 treat?

CryoSkin 3.0 is used for fat reduction, skin tightening, cellulite smoothing, and facial work. Common body areas include abdomen, flanks, thighs, arms, glutes, back, chest, and jawline.

What is the abdominal contouring option?

It is a separate focused visit for de-puffing, stubborn-area support, and body-contouring goals that do not necessarily need a full CryoSkin fat-reduction appointment.

How many CryoSkin sessions do I need?

It depends on the goal. Fat reduction, skin tightening, cellulite smoothing, and facial work are planned differently, so Dan will map the area and recommend the right cadence before you commit.

Can I book just a cryofacial?

Absolutely. There is a CryoSkin 3.0 facial option and a localized liquid nitrogen cryofacial option. Dan can help you choose the better fit for de-puffing, tightening, glow, or event prep.

Will I see cryofacial results right away?

Some clients notice a refreshed or less puffy appearance after a visit, but timing and results vary. Dan can talk through what is realistic for a single visit or a short series.
