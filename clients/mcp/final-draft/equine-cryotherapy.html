Equine Cryotherapy

Equine cryotherapy for training load and recovery support.

This page keeps the horse-specific material separate so the pricing, use cases, and barn-day logistics stay clear.

Request an Equine Visit View Equine Pricing

Who it is for

Sport horses, working horses, aging horses, trainers, and private owners who want recovery support without hauling out for it.

The old site framed this around post-training recovery, joint stiffness support, tendon and ligament stress, swelling, fluid retention, and general comfort during heavier workload periods.

Common areas

Knees or carpus, hocks or tarsus, fetlocks, pasterns, cannon bones, flexor tendons, suspensory regions, shoulders, back, hindquarters, hamstrings, and neck.

Common use cases

After training, after competition, during heavier workload blocks, for ongoing maintenance, or for aging horses that benefit from extra recovery support.

Positioning

This complements veterinary and farrier care. It does not replace diagnosis, veterinary treatment, or rehabilitation planning.
