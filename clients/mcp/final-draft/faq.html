Cryotherapy Basics

What this is and what it is not.

Is this whole-body cryotherapy?

No. Mobile Cryo Pro focuses on localized cryotherapy and CryoSkin 3.0 protocols rather than a chamber-based whole-body format.

What does localized cryotherapy usually help support?

People usually book it for inflammation support, mobility comfort, overworked tissue, post-training recovery, and recurring hot spots that keep flaring up.

Recovery

Questions people ask before they book pain-support or recovery sessions.

What areas do people usually treat?

Common requests include shoulders, knees, hips, lower back, feet, elbows, plantar-fascia irritation, repetitive-strain zones, and post-workout soreness.

How often do recovery sessions usually happen?

That depends on the goal. Some clients book a one-off visit, while others use a short package when the issue is recurring or the training load is high.

Body Contouring + CryoSkin

Questions around contour refinement, CryoSkin Slim, Tone, and facial-focused work.

What areas are usually booked for contour work?

Abdomen, flanks, waistline, thighs, arms, buttocks, back, neck, and jawline support are the most common zones.

What counts as one treatment area?

One area usually means one continuous zone. Larger or mirrored zones can count as more than one area depending on size and symmetry.

How often are contour packages usually booked?

The old site pointed toward structured weekly or intensive package rhythms. This version keeps it simple: contour work is usually handled as a package rather than a random one-off.

Can cryofacials be booked on their own?

Yes. Cryofacials can be booked as standalone visits or add-ons depending on the location, timing, and the rest of the appointment.

Mobile Appointments + Equine

Questions about logistics, service area, and horse-specific bookings.

Where can appointments happen?

Homes, gyms, hotels, workplaces, wineries, private properties, barns, and hosted events across Sonoma County and Marin, with select North Bay requests by request.

Do you offer equine cryotherapy?

Yes. Equine sessions are available by request, with separate pricing, separate booking context, and barn-day options for multi-horse setups.

Can I book a hotel, office, or event activation?

Yes. These are quoted around timing, location, and the number of people involved.

Can I just call or text instead of using the form?

Absolutely. The booking page is simply the cleanest way to send the service, location, and timing in one shot.
