FAQ

A few cleaned-up questions people usually ask before they book.

Is this whole-body cryotherapy?

No. Mobile Cryo Pro focuses on localized cryotherapy and CryoSkin 3.0 protocols, so treatment is applied to targeted areas rather than through a full-body chamber.

What kinds of things do people book localized recovery sessions for?

Common requests include knees, shoulders, hips, low back, feet, elbows, post-workout soreness, repetitive strain, and inflammation-prone areas that keep flaring up after training, work, or day-to-day overuse.

How often do contour or recovery packages usually run?

That depends on the goal. Recovery clients often book a short series closer together, while contour work is usually handled as weekly or structured package appointments with a specific area count and timeline.

Where can appointments happen?

Most visits happen at homes, gyms, hotels, offices, wineries, barns, and private events across Sonoma County and Marin, with select North Bay bookings by request.

Do you offer equine cryotherapy as a separate service?

Yes. Equine appointments have their own page, pricing, and barn rates, and are available by request for sport, working, and aging horses.
