Localized Cryotherapy

Recovery and pain support for one specific area.

This is the service people book for sore joints, overworked tissue, inflammation-prone areas, and post-workout recovery.

  • Common areas include knees, shoulders, hips, lower back, feet, and elbows
  • Often booked for flare-ups, repetitive strain, and training recovery
  • Available as a single visit or a short package
