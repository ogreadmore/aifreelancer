Mobile cryotherapy after surgery, when your clinician says it fits.

Private recovery

A calmer appointment at your location.

After surgery, travel and waiting rooms can be the last thing you want. Mobile Cryo Pro brings a clean, focused setup to homes, hotels, and recovery spaces across Sonoma, Marin, and nearby North Bay communities.

Focused areas

Target the area that needs support.

Localized cryotherapy can be directed around a specific treatment area rather than exposing the whole body to cold. Common requests involve knees, shoulders, hips, feet, back tension, and areas affected by soreness.

Clinical common sense

We work around your recovery instructions.

Post-surgical recovery is personal. If you have restrictions, wound-care instructions, numbness, circulation concerns, or a complex medical history, confirm with your clinician before booking.

Follow-through

Good for one visit or a recovery series.

Some clients book one focused visit, while others schedule a short series during recovery. Dan can help think through timing, location, and whether the mobile setup makes sense for your situation.

Questions

Before booking after surgery.

Should I ask my doctor first?

Yes. After surgery, your clinician’s recovery instructions come first. Mobile Cryo Pro can work within appropriate guidance.

Can you come to my home?

Yes. Home visits are often the point, especially when travel is inconvenient during recovery.

Is this only for athletes?

No. Many clients are older adults, active adults, or people trying to stay comfortable and mobile during normal recovery.
