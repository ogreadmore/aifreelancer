The story

Good tools, in the right hands, on your schedule.

I got into cryotherapy because it works. I kept going because the people I was helping—athletes, busy parents, horse owners, folks getting ready for weddings— kept getting real results.

The one thing missing? A clinic visit that didn’t feel like a clinic visit. So I built a mobile setup that brings everything to you: the gear, the expertise, the care. No waiting rooms, no hurried check-ins, no up-sells.

It’s just me and the client—and that’s the whole point.
