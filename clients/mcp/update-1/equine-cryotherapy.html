Who it helps

From weekend trail horses to weekend competitors.

Eventers, jumpers, dressage mounts, reiners, cow horses, endurance horses, and the trail partner who’s earned easier days. We help with recovery, soft-tissue stress, and keeping older horses comfortable.

Where it works

Knees, hocks, fetlocks, pasterns, cannon bones, flexor tendons, suspensories, shoulders, back, hindquarters, and neck.

When to book

After hard rides. During competition season. Before and after long hauls. As a regular thing for aging horses.

Friendly with the team

We work alongside your vet, farrier, chiro, and body worker—never in place of them.
