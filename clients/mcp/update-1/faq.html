The basics

How this all works.

Is this whole-body cryotherapy?

Nope. We do localized cryotherapy and CryoSkin—no big chamber required. That’s why we can come to you.

What does a visit actually look like?

Dan shows up, sets up in about ten minutes, and we have a quick chat about what you’re working on. Treatment itself takes 15–45 minutes depending on the service. You’re back to your day right after.

Is it safe?

Yes—it’s non-invasive and drug-free. We do a quick intake to flag anything that needs extra care (cold sensitivity, certain circulatory conditions, pregnancy). When in doubt, we’ll ask you to check with your doctor first.

Recovery

For sore bodies.

What can it help with?

Sore shoulders, knees, hips, backs, elbows, feet. Plantar fasciitis, tennis elbow, rotator-cuff flare-ups, post-workout soreness—the usual suspects that get in the way of training and daily life.

How fast will I feel something?

Most people feel a difference after the first visit. For bigger goals, a short series (3–10 sessions) works best.

Does this replace PT or medical care?

Not at all. We pair well with physical therapy, chiropractic, massage, and your regular care team. If something’s outside our lane, we’ll tell you.

CryoSkin & cryofacials

For the aesthetic stuff.

What can CryoSkin treat?

Abdomen, flanks, thighs, arms, glutes, back, chest, and jawline. It’s great for contouring, firming, and smoothing cellulite.

What counts as one area?

One continuous zone. Both thighs? That’s two areas. A full abdomen? One. We’ll map it out with you and quote the whole thing before anything starts.

How many CryoSkin sessions do I need?

Most people do 5, 7, or 10—spaced 1–2 weeks apart. We’ll take before-and-after photos and measurements so you can see the change.

Can I book just a cryofacial?

Absolutely. It’s a favorite before weddings, travel, or big days—but also just on its own. Pairs well with any other service, too.

Will I see cryofacial results right away?

Yes. The de-puff and glow show up within the hour. Do a short series and the effect sticks around longer between visits.
