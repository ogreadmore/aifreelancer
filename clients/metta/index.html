The method

One practice, four movements

1

Find it

Bring to mind the being who evokes the strongest feeling of love in you. No anchor is too humble — the love you can actually feel is worth more than the love you think you should feel.

2

Strengthen it

Give the feeling your full attention and turn it all the way up. Think of metta as a mantra you feel rather than say.

3

Expand it

Keep the same felt wish and let it include more: yourself, a neutral person, a difficult person, and finally all beings — above, below, and all around.

4

Return to it

Standing, walking, sitting, lying down. When you notice it has slipped away, return. Each return makes it more familiar, until it is simply there.

Morning: twenty minutes. During the day: countless returns. Night: five minutes in bed.
