Orbit Drift

A classic vector-style space shooter built for the browser. Drift through a rock field, split the larger asteroids, and keep your ship alive long enough to chase a local high score.

Controls

  • Rotate with A/D or the left and right arrows
  • Thrust with W or the up arrow
  • Fire with Space or hold the Fire button
  • Pause with P and restart with R

Every ship, asteroid, spark, and star is drawn locally with canvas. No copied sprites, no external game assets.

Drift field

Stay moving, use short bursts of thrust, and split the rocks before they box you in.

Keyboard: arrows or A/D to steer, W or Up to thrust, Space to fire, P to pause, R to restart.
