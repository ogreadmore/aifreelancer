Crossing guide

Quick controls and a few things to watch for.

How to play Move one hop at a time, reach the top curb, then keep going as stage speed ramps up. Each crash costs a life, so use the lanes carefully.

Arrow keys or WASD move the hopper. Space pauses or resumes. On phones and tablets, tap the buttons or swipe on the road to hop.
